ABOUT US

The MONOKEN is an organization established in 2013 by some students. Until now, there have been several activities for students to do manufacturing at our school, but there was no organization for flexibly researching and developing based on individual purposes. Therefore, MONOKEN is for the students who would like to try things that no one has ever done before. Currently, there are 4 fourth-year students, 2 third-year students, 3 second-year students, and 3 first-year students (12 students in total).
The two events in which MONOKEN presents its results are the Kobe City College of Technology Festival and the National Design Competition(全国デザコン). We are working hard every day to prepare for these events, which are held every fall.

WORKS

Some of our works are available to the public (also on Instagram). We work basically according to individual purposes, so each work has characteristics from 3D printers to robots. We will keep trying to research and developing of various works which have full of ideas.
